Intro

Are you a DevOps specialist looking to share your knowledge and learn from other experts in the field? Look no further than DevOps in Shorts, a lightning talk conference dedicated to bringing together the brightest minds in DevOps for a day of learning and collaboration.

What are lightning talks, you ask? They’re brief, 10-15 minute talks that provide a high-level overview of a topic, idea, or project. These short-form talks are perfect for sharing knowledge and sparking conversations with other DevOps professionals.

At DevOps in Shorts, you’ll have the opportunity to hear from a variety of speakers on a range of topics, including automation, containerization, continuous integration and delivery, infrastructure as code, and much more. Whether you’re a seasoned DevOps pro or just starting out in the field, you’re sure to learn something new and valuable.
